Everything starts with a context

Preparing a meeting, answering a customer, understanding a project, or simply looking for information. All these situations are as many different "contexts" that require a global understanding of the situation. Today, we gather information from all our data sources and applications using search.

We search through all we have

We search trough emails, files, cloud services, local applications. This takes time and is mostly inefficient. In a business context, the relevant information in not where we expect it to be. The last Email may never contain the google drive or dropbox link that you need. You may also not know what to search and especially you do not want to spend time searching it. That is why information has to come to us firsthand, without search.

The solution is contextual

Contextual means that whatever your job is, you (the user) get all and only the information you need. It means that you don't need to switch applications or use another search or aggregation app. You get a contextual feed directly within the application or software you are using. In the end it's efficiency in your relationships, perfect meeting preparation and powering data into an asset.
